CheckIfMovedThis proc is called every time the Helix is about to fire its lasers. It's responsible for populating the list of target turfs for its laser barrages. Why does this exist? On the off chance someone does something EXTREMELY cursed with a W Corp teleporter OR an admin moves it somewhere forcibly. This proc will ensure the Helix is always firing lasers in the correct general area, and it will also move the shell objects to it to make sure it looks right.
