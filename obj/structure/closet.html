closet
/obj/structure/closet

This file serves to give the closets the functionality of making the mobs inside of them not necessarily safe When going inside of a locker, a 5x5 area around you gets you into their var/list/possible_hiding_players That list can aggro a mob when the mob walks over that tile onto a player inside of that list depending on var/noise

Vars

divableWhether a skittish person can dive inside this closet. Disable if opening the closet causes "bad things" to happen or that it leads to a logical inconsistency.
show_breathingif TRUE, colors tiles that are affected by people breathing whilst hiding in containers

Procs

after_closeProc to override for effects after closing a door
after_openProc to override for effects after opening a door
toggle_breathingToggles the turf's noise and possible_hiding_players list contents
