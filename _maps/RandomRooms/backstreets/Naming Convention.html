Maps that are "Generic" like generic apartments, medical centres, etc, should be named as following

Genericname_(size),(direction),(letter)

For instance
apartment_mec

Is a Medium map, on the EAST side of the hallway (Not opens eastwards, is on the east) and is the 3rd map with this generic name.```
